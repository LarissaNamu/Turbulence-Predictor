Welcome to Turbulence Predictor!

For American Airlines

Our inspiration for this project came from 2 of our developers' fear of airplane turbulence (ooo scary).
Many American Airlines passengers experience the discomfort of unexpected turbulence each year.
So 4 beginner developers from this year's TAMU Hack decided to take a stand and create a program that maps
zones in a plane's route that allows passengers to be informed about when turbulence may occur.
